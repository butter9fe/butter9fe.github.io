About
Ho Li Lian

I am an aspiring game developer based in Singapore, having recently graduated from Nanyang Polytechnic's Diploma in Game Development and Technology.

Ever since I modded a minigame for a simple flash game when I was 9 years old, I have fallen in love with programming. I am absolutely fascinated with how typing a series of words on the screen can create an entire world that players can immerse themselves in. Becoming a game developer is my dream job that combines both of my favorite past-times.

To date, I have learned 8 programming languages, created 9 indie games, and worked with 2 development-related companies. Through my work experience, I have earned a reputation as a "jack of all trades" who can pick up new skills quickly and deliver high-quality features fast.

In my free time, you can find me playing MMORPGs, watching anime, drawing, or even creating a new game as a passion project.
